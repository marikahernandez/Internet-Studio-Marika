1. Shibuya Crossing

Shibuya crossing is a famous intersection in Tokyo, Japan that is featured in many hit anime series showing the city's lively culture and fast pace of the city. This intersection is a huge symbol of Tokyo, with its neon video screens and modern aesthetics that help bring out the beauty of Tokyo.

2. Tokyo Skytree

The tokyo skytree is the highest tower in tokyo, which gives that beautiful view of tokyos skyline. This isn't a super important place in the anime, but rather a place that can allow you to view the beauty of Tokyo from a higher view.

3. Kaneki's University

Kamii University is the fictional university in the Tokyo Ghoul Series, that is heavily inspired by the real world institution Rikkyo University. The characters Ken Kaneki, along with his best friend Hideyoshi Nagachika, and his ghoul rival Nishiki Nishio attend this universtiy in the series.
