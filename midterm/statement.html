For my webpage design, I wanted to follow the themes of vampire, gothic and ghoulish vibes that are represented in the anime. I am really drawn towards the gothic darker designs in general with color scales of black, red, whites, and grays and this anime does a great job of potraying those color scales in the aesthetics of the design. For the visuals of my design, I wanted all my colors to match with the aesthetic potrayed in the anime. I really like the darker colors and knew I wanted to incorporate them into my designs.

For the interaction part of my design I wanted to make it super simple and easy to navigate. I liked the buttons style from when we did it in class, so I followed the same codes to help me create those for my webage. Each button is a easy way to navigate through to each page so this was the best way to help keep my design interactive but also simple.
